Ms. Carter's Electronic Gradebook

Python

This Electronic Gradebook System project is designed to help solve the everyday challenges faced by teachers like Ms. Carter, who manages multiple classes and over 150 students. The system automates grading, tracks assignment submissions, monitors student performance, and generates progress reports.

It provides real-time grade updates for students and parents, reduces manual grade calculations, and identifies struggling students early so teachers can provide timely support.

Ramify AI

AI

Ramify AI is an intelligent educational support app for Winston-Salem State University students. It centralizes academic resources, personalizes learning plans, and connects students with tutoring, advising, and support services.

The app uses data and AI to map major requirements, break down syllabi, generate customized study materials, and support academic decision-making through smart advising.
